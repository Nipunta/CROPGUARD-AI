## üìä Understanding Results

### Feasibility Score
- **90-100%**: Highly recommended, easy to implement
- **70-89%**: Good option, some challenges
- **50-69%**: Possible but difficult
- **Below 50%**: Not recommended for your situation

### Payback Period
- **1-3 months**: Very quick returns
- **4-6 months**: Reasonable timeline
- **7-12 months**: Medium-term investment
- **12+ months**: Long-term investment

### Effort Level
- **Low**: Minimal work, can do alone
- **Medium**: Some effort, may need help
- **High**: Significant work, need training/support
