### 1. Crop Residue Management (CRM) Scheme
**Implementing Agency:** Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

**Subsidy Details:**
- **Individual Farmers:** 50% subsidy (up to ‚Çπ1,00,000)
- **Farmer Cooperatives:** 80% subsidy (up to ‚Çπ10,00,000)
- **Custom Hiring Centers:** 80% subsidy (up to ‚Çπ10,00,000)

**Eligible Equipment:**
- Super Straw Management System (SMS)
- Happy Seeder
- Paddy Straw Chopper
- Rotary Slasher
- Mulcher
- Zero Till Drill
- Baler (round/square)
- Rake

**How to Apply:**
1. Visit District Agriculture Office
2. Submit application with required documents
3. Get quotation from authorized dealer
4. Approval within 15-30 days
5. Purchase equipment and submit bills
6. Subsidy credited to bank account

**Contact:**
- Helpline: 1800-180-1551
- Website: https://agricoop.nic.in
- Email: agri-coop@nic.in

### 2. PM-KUSUM Scheme (Component C)
**Implementing Agency:** Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE)

**Subsidy Details:**
- 60% subsidy on solar pumps and equipment
- 30% from Central Government
- 30% from State Government
- 40% farmer contribution (loan available)

**Applicable For:**
- Biogas plants with solar integration
- Biomass processing units
- Solar-powered composting units

**How to Apply:**
1. Visit State Nodal Agency office
2. Online application at https://pmkusum.mnre.gov.in
3. Submit feasibility report
4. Get technical approval
5. Install equipment through empaneled vendor
6. Inspection and subsidy release

**Contact:**
- MNRE Helpline: 011-2436-0707
- Email: info@mnre.gov.in
- Website: https://pmkusum.mnre.gov.in

### 3. National Biofuel Policy - Biomass Price Support
**Implementing Agency:** Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

**Benefits:**
- Minimum Support Price: ‚Çπ2,000-3,000 per ton
- Direct purchase by OMC-registered plants
- No middleman involvement
- Guaranteed payment

**Eligible Biomass:**
- Rice straw/stubble
- Wheat straw
- Cotton stalks
- Sugarcane trash
- Maize stalks

**How to Sell:**
1. Find nearest registered biomass collection center
2. Contact plant directly (list on petroleum.nic.in)
3. Deliver baled biomass
4. Get weighment slip
5. Receive payment within 7 days

**Contact:**
- Website: https://petroleum.nic.in

## üìù How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

### For Individual Farmers

**Step 1: Preparation**
- Decide which equipment you need
- Check eligibility criteria
- Gather required documents

**Step 2: Application**
- Visit District Agriculture Office
- Fill application form (available online/offline)
- Attach all required documents
- Submit to concerned officer

**Step 3: Quotation**
- Get quotation from authorized dealer
- Submit to Agriculture Department
- Wait for approval (15-30 days)

**Step 4: Purchase**
- Buy equipment after approval
- Keep all original bills and receipts
- Get delivery challan

**Step 5: Claim Subsidy**
- Submit bills to Agriculture Office
- Provide bank account details
- Subsidy credited within 30-60 days

---

### For Farmer Cooperatives/CHCs

**Additional Requirements:**
- Society registration certificate
- List of member farmers
- Resolution for equipment purchase
- Business plan (for CHCs)

**Higher Subsidy:** 80% instead of 50%

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Q1: How long does subsidy approval take?
**A:** Typically 15-30 days after document submission. May vary by state.

### Q2: Can I buy equipment before approval?
**A:** No. You must get approval first, then purchase. Otherwise subsidy will be rejected.

### Q3: Is subsidy available for used/second-hand equipment?
**A:** No. Subsidy is only for new equipment from authorized dealers.

### Q4: Can I apply for multiple equipment?
**A:** Yes, but total subsidy is capped (‚Çπ1 lakh for individuals).

### Q5: What if my application is rejected?
**A:** You can reapply after correcting issues. Contact Agriculture Office for reasons.

### Q6: Is there subsidy for renting equipment?
**A:** No direct subsidy for rental. But Custom Hiring Centers get 80% subsidy.

### Q7: Can tenant farmers apply?
**A:** Yes, with landowner's consent letter and lease agreement.

### Q8: How is subsidy paid?
**A:** Direct Bank Transfer (DBT) to your registered bank account.

### Q9: What if equipment breaks down?
**A:** Warranty from manufacturer. Subsidy doesn't cover repairs.

### Q10: Can I sell subsidized equipment?
**A:** Not for 7 years. If sold, subsidy must be returned.
